Semesterly – Course Scheduling App

Semesterly is a web app that allows students to easily add, view, and manage their courses in a calendar format. Designed to address the common pain points of academic scheduling, it provides an intuitive, visual tool for students.

Case Study

Problem Statement

Many students struggle to organize and visualize their course schedules, leading to missed deadlines and scheduling conflicts. Semesterly addresses this by providing an intuitive, visual scheduling tool.

Intended Solution

A web app that allows students to easily add, view, and manage their courses in a calendar format.

Target Audience

College and university students seeking a better way to manage their academic schedules.

User Research

We conducted surveys and interviews with students to understand their scheduling pain points and needs.

Quantitative Data Summary

Typical Course Planning Method

Students typically use a combination of paper planners, spreadsheets, and university portals, which can be cumbersome and error-prone.
